The Pluto wood watch is made from 100% natural ebony, maple, red sandalwood, and verawood. Japanese movement and the wood strap is adjustable in size. Because the wood grain pattern is naturally different each time, every wood watch is different as well. This natural variation in wood grain is an attractive feature of these wooden timepieces that every possessor of these will feel the uniqueness of it. 

At Bewell, we take great pride to our workmanship and provide great service to our customers.

  • Ship next business day with gift message on card as needed
  • Watch and watch band rendered in real wood
  • Japanese quartz movement 
  • Designed with calendar
  • Stainless steel tri-fold clasp with push buttons 
  • Water resistance 3 ATM

Specification: 

  • Case diameter:46mm
  • Case thickness: 10mm
  • Band length: 220mm
  • Band width: 24mm
