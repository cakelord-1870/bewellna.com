The Cupid Minimalist wood watch is made from 100% natural ebony, maple, red sandalwood, and zebrawood. Japanese movement and the wood strap is adjustable in size.  Because the wood grain pattern is naturally different each piece, every wood watch is different as well. This natural variation in wood grain is an attractive feature of these wooden timepieces that every possessor of these will feel the uniqueness of it. 

At Bewell, we take great pride to our workmanship and serve our customers.

  • Ship next business day with gift message as needed
  • Watch and watch band rendered in real wood
  • Minimalist design
  • Japanese quartz movement 
  • Designed with calendar and luminous dial
  • Stainless steel tri-fold clasp with push buttons 

Specification: 

  • Dial Diameter: 43mm
  • Case thickness: 9mm
  • Band Length: 220mm
  • Band Width: 20mm
